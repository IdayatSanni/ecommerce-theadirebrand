Our Story

The Adire brand started in 2019 and was inspired by my parents specifically mother. I grew up seeing majority of her outfits to be Adire as she is a true lover of it. So, I felt why not bring to light the beauty of our culture, not only as a Yoruba woman but also African in influencing your style and fashion. At The Adire Brand, we have picked the best patterns that will match and exceed modern fashion trends. As such our aim is to exceed your expectations by providing you with quality, affordable and luxurious prints.
