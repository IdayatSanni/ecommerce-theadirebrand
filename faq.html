F.A.Q

Shipping

We offer both nationwide and international shipping. Standard shipping rate of ₦1000 across Abeokuta excluding outskirts such as Aiyetoro, Obada Oko, Aro, Ilese Awo, Oke Ata and beyond at ₦1500.

Delivery

All orders will be processed and packed within one-two working day of your order being placed and payment confirmed. Our official logistics company is DHL which often take within 1-3 working days, but remote areas can take a little longer. We only accept full payment before delivery. Payment verifies ORDER.

RETURN/REFUND

We are proud to offer our customers quality products. However, should a product be damaged or faulty at the time of delivery, please contact us at theadirebrand@gmail.com. The order number from your original confirmation email, a description of the complaint, e-mail address and phone number is very important. Once we have received all essential details, an exchange of the damaged product will be processed. In a situation whereby the damaged product is out of stock, a full refund of the product paid will be authorized.
